Document Services

Reliable support for important personal documentation processes.

In Ghana, document applications such as passports, Ghana Cards, and driver’s licences usually require specific identity records and supporting documents, so careful preparation helps reduce delays and avoidable errors.

AJ Consult provides hands-on support for people who need practical help with document processing, record updates, and application preparation.

Our goal is to help you understand requirements clearly, organise your paperwork properly, and approach each process with better confidence and accuracy.
